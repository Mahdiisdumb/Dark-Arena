# Dark Arena

Dark Arena is a physics-driven VR experience built in Unity where combat isn’t about hacking enemies to pieces — it’s about controlling space.

Instead of traditional fighting mechanics, you use your sword to **deflect, redirect, and fling enemies away**. Precision, timing, and physics matter more than button mashing.

This is controlled chaos in a dark environment.

---

## Concept

Dark Arena focuses on:

- Physics-based interaction
- Sword-driven force redirection
- Enemy manipulation rather than direct damage
- Immersive VR movement
- Atmospheric dark environments

You don’t just swing.
You redirect momentum.

---

## Core Gameplay

- Enemies approach the player
- The sword applies force and momentum
- Proper timing allows you to fling enemies across the arena
- Arena space management becomes the core challenge

The goal isn’t destruction.
It’s control.
